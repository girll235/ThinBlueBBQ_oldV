# ThinBlueBBQ_oldV

Fire up your screens for Thin Blue BBQ – where the sizzle of authentic Texas-style barbecue meets live-streamed pit action and instant Instagram cravings, all in one mouthwatering web spot!

## About Thin Blue BBQ

Step into the smoky heart of Texas barbecue at Thin Blue BBQ, a family-run spot serving up tender brisket, fall-off-the-bone ribs, and all the fixings that bring folks together. Whether you're craving that slow-smoked flavor or just soaking in the vibe of backyard gatherings, this site pulls the pit right to your device. No reservations needed – just dive into the digital aroma of real-deal BBQ, from pit-master magic to social media shares that keep the hunger alive.

## Key Features

- **Live Stream**: Watch the grill in real time – catch the flames dancing and meats turning golden as the pros work their magic. It's like front-row seats to the sizzle!
- **Instagram Feed**: Scroll endless pics of juicy plates, behind-the-scenes stories, and customer feasts with a single click. Tag your own BBQ dreams and join the flavor party.

## Getting Started

Fire it up in seconds – no fancy setup required. This site's built to load and go, perfect for peeking at the action from anywhere.

1. Clone the repo:  
   ```
   git clone https://github.com/girll235/ThinBlueBBQ_oldV.git
   ```
2. Open `index.html` in your favorite browser. Boom – you're grilling vicariously!
3. Dive in: Hit play on the embedded live stream to see the pit live, or tap the Instagram widget for a feed of mouthwatering moments. (Pro tip: Best viewed on desktop for that full-screen smoke show.)

Want to host it yourself? Drop the files on any web server or GitHub Pages for easy sharing.
